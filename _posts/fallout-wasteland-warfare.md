# Intro

There are many items available for the [Modiphius Fallout Games](https://modiphius.net/collections/fallout), some items are available to be purchased as physical items or as a digital download and sometimes both.

Due to the abscence of a Buyer's Guide, I have created this page to try to understand the links between items so that I know what to purchase and as a place to track the items I am either interested in or own.

# Fallout: Wasteland Warfare

`Fallout: Wasteland Warfare` is a tabletop miniatures wargame for the Fallout Series.

The [Release Tracker of items released in waves 1-7](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0225/4035/files/Fallout_Release_Tracker_-_121022.pdf) does not have release dates for waves 1-4,
I have estimated these by sifting through the Modiphius [Blog Posts](https://modiphius.net/blogs/news), [Press Releases](https://modiphius.net/blogs/press-releases) and Store on the [Internet Archive Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org).

## Wave 1 - Starter Set

Wave 1 introduces the game with the `Two Player Starter Set` which contains everything you need to learn how to play the game.
