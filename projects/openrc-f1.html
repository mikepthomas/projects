Tamiya F104 Hollow, Front Hard Tyre2Rubber£13.27 (Set of 2)

To make the tyres soft enough to be usable, they need to have a very low percentage of infil, therefore they are quite fragile and can be easily split.

To get better grip and have harder wearing tyres, it's recommended to use off-the-shelf rubber tyres specifically desgned for radio controlled cars.

Electronics Box

This was one of the first items I modified. I used SketchUp 2017 to edit an existing electronics box to fit the battery I purchased. You can also view the design on the SketchUp website.

The source file and STL is available to download on my 3D Printing repository on GitHub.
