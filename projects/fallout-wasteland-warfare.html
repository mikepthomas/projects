Intro

There are many items available for the Modiphius Fallout Games, some items are available to be purchased as physical items or as a digital download and sometimes both.

Due to the abscence of a Buyer's Guide, I have created this page to try to understand the links between items so that I know what to purchase and as a place to track the items I am either interested in or own.

Fallout: Wasteland Warfare

Fallout: Wasteland Warfare is a tabletop miniatures wargame for the Fallout Series.

The Release Tracker of items released in waves 1-7 does not have release dates for waves 1-4, I have estimated these by sifting through the Modiphius Blog Posts, Press Releases and Store on the Internet Archive Wayback Machine.

Wave 1 - Starter Set

Wave 1 introduces the game with the Two Player Starter Set which contains everything you need to learn how to play the game.
