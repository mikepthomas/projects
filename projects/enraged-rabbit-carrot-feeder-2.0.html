Intro

Building a the second version of the Enraged Rabbit Carrot Feeder to print using multiple colours of filament on the Voron.

Enraged Rabbit Carrot Feeder

For V2.0 the recommended firmware is Happy Hare and there is also a fork of KlipperScreen, KlipperScreen Happy Hare Edition.
