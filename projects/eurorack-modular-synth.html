Intro

I am a fan of Look Mum No Computer, In one of his videos he mentioned about the website Music From Outer Space and the book Make: Analog Synthesizers which I have brought and I am currently reading through.

Controller

I wanted something that I could connect up to a computer via USB to use software synths like VCVRack or for connecting up to Garageband and maybe later Logic Pro. I also wanted something that is able to control hardware synths with Pitch CV, Velocity CV and Gate control or via MIDI inputs and outputs. The Keystep Pro provides all 3, via USB, MIDI and mono 3.5mm output jacks.

ItemQuantityReceivedNotes
KeyStep Pro11The Special Black Edition I purchased also came with 14 CV/Gate cables for connecting to the eurorack modules
